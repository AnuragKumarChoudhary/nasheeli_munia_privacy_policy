Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Nasheeli Munia Gaming" app ("we", "our", "us") uses information when it connects to Facebook to publish videos and captions on behalf of a Facebook Page.

Information we access from Facebook

With your permission, our app may access:

  • Basic Page information (Page ID, name)
  • Permissions needed to publish content on the Page (e.g. posts and videos)

We use this information only to automatically upload videos and captions to the selected Facebook Page. We do not sell this data or share it with third parties.

Data storage

The app stores only technical configuration data needed to connect to Facebook (such as access tokens) on the server or local machine where the script runs. No personal user data is stored beyond what is required to operate the app.

Your choices

You can revoke the app's access to your Facebook account or Page at any time from your Facebook settings. Once access is revoked, the app will no longer be able to read or publish content on your behalf.
